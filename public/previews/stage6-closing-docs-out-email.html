📋 Action Needed: Read below and prepare for closing!

➡️ Next Step: Loan Funding!

If You're Receiving Funds: Bring a voided check from the account you'd like your funds wired to.

If You're Bringing Funds to Close: Be sure to either wire the funds in advance or bring a cashier's check from your bank. (Personal checks are not accepted at closing.)

ID Required: Bring a valid driver's license (or other government-issued photo ID).

Everyone on Title Must Attend: Make sure all parties listed on the title are present for closing and able to sign in person.

When to Expect Your Funds:

  • Purchase: Immediately!
  • Refinance/2nd Lien/HELOC and Primary Residence: Funds are disbursed on the 4th business day after closing (Saturdays count!).
  • Second Homes or Investment Properties: Funds are typically wired same day or next business day, depending on your closing time.
  • For Virginia Properties: Virginia law requires that we fund only after the county records your deed of trust.
